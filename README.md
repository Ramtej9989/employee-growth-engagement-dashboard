## Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides insights into employee growth, performance, engagement, training participation, promotions, and attrition trends to support data-driven HR decision-making.

## Key KPIs
- Total Employees
- Average Performance Score
- Training Participation Rate
- Promotion Rate by Gender
- Attrition Rate
- Average Training Hours

## Dashboard Features
- Employee growth by hire year
- Promotion trends by department
- Performance analysis by department and category
- Training participation by department and training type
- Attrition analysis by tenure band
- Career level distribution
- Gender-based promotion and attrition insights

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX
- SQL
- Excel
- HR Analytics

## Data
- Mock HR dataset created for portfolio use
- Employee data anonymized
- Structured and optimized for reporting

## Key Insights
- Majority of employees fall within Junior and Mid career levels
- Training participation remains high across departments
- Attrition is higher during early tenure periods
- Performance scores are consistent across departments
